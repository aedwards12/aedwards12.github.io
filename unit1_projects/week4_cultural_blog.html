My Conflict 18 May 2014

In life it is only natural to have conflict with someone. It is how we handle this conflict that we can truly learn and grow from it. I have an on going conflict that doesn’t deal with a specific person but rather myself. All my life i loved to dance, but when it comes to dancing solo in front of my peers. I panic and forget the choreography. What is starting to happen is, when the teacher starts looking at students to call out to perform the days choreography, I begin to hide in the back of the class and become withdrawn. This is something a battle with because i really want to perform in front of the class and show my skills. When i dance with a group I perform great because i know that all eyes aren’t on me. I don’t know why i feel this way. Maybe i am afraid of being judged or maybe I’m not good enough. I think i shy away from my skills when i know there is someone better than me in the crowd. I think I need to face this conflict by starting to dance more in the front of the class. and slowly start dancing in front of my friends until i feel confident to dance in front of the class. It is nice to write this blog and official write about it. This is something I think about all the time when I take a dance class. I really need to find a way to stop withdrawing and face the issue head on.
