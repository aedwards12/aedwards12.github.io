Problems facing technology 25 May 2014

As technology continues to grow our devices are beginning to store more and more information about ourselves. Some of this information is considered sensitive, such as passwords, address, username, social security numbers, credit card numbers ect. This information needs to be kept in a secure environment free from hacker attacks. Cybersecurity is the body of technologies, processes and practices designed to protect networks, computers, programs and data from attack, damage or unauthorized access. Ensuring cybersecurity requires coordinated efforts throughout an information system. Elements of cybersecurity include:

  • Application security
  • Information security
  • Network security
  • Disaster recovery / business continuity planning
